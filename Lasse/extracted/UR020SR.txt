User Requirement 020:
  STATEMENT In a voice- and video-conference a user shall be able to turn on/off his sound-input, -output and video-input
    PRIORITY B
		
System Requirement 020.1:
	STATEMENT A voice- and video-conference plugin shall provide several buttons
		PRIORITY B
		
System Requirement 020.2:
	STATEMENT One of those buttons shall provide the function to toggle sound recording.
		PRIORITY B
		
System Requirement 020.3:
	STATEMENT One of those buttons shall provide the function to toggle video recording.
		PRIORITY B
		
System Requirement 020.4:
	STATEMENT One of those buttons shall provide the function to toggle sound output from the other participants.
		PRIORITY B
